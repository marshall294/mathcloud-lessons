Long Division - Step-by-Step with Diagrams

Worked Example 1: Divide 425 by 5

Step 1: Take the first two digits (42).
Step 2: 5 goes into 42 eight times. Write down 8 as the first digit of the quotient.
Step 3: Multiply 8 × 5 = 40. Subtract 40 from 42 to get 2.
Step 4: Bring down the next digit (5) to make 25.
Step 5: 5 goes into 25 five times. Write down 5 as the next digit of the quotient.
Step 6: Multiply 5 × 5 = 25. Subtract to get 0. No digits left, so the division is complete.
Answer: 425 ÷ 5 = 85
